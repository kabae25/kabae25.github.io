Browse and Download EssentialsX

Download EssentialsX.

EssentialsX is a quality of life plugin that adds a variety of features like /home, /tpa, and more useful survival-centric commands.

  1. Navigate to https://github.com/EssentialsX/Essentials/releases.
  2. Scroll down to the Assets section and download EssentialsX-2.20.1.jar
    Tip: Depending on your version, 2.20.1 may vary. This is the version indicator.
  3. If prompted to save, do so in the Downloads folder of your computer

Results: EssentialsX has been installed
